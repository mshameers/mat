About Matrix Trading Company

Matrix is an interior design firm specialized in corporate and commercial interior environments. Since 2007, Matrix has showcased a collaborative culture that encourages open relationships between our designers and our clients; relationships based on trust, respect, communication and teamwork.. We learned that success is attained through respect, understanding and cohesiveness, and is proud to lead a dedicated and professional team of experts that guides on their collective mission to consistently exceed client expectations.

Sustainability

Matrix is committed to the responsibility of developing green, sustainable design and principles in all our projects. Our designers understand the impact of their decisions on the environment and always specify sustainable elements.

To create a better well designed Living or Work space that people can afford.
